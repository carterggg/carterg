IMMINENT RADIO

WHAT?
A simple site with a Soundcloud playlist of all the songs and sometimes even whole albums of upcoming artist that give me good vibes and listens. Music that is 'Bound to Happen.'

ROLE?
Creator, Graphic and Web Designer, Music selector.

WHY?
Life without music? No.

How?
I got into music more when I was around fifteen years old after my dad showed me MF DOOM and DJ Dangermouse’s album “DANGERDOOM.” From there on I dropped the radio and started listening to different music genres, music that if Beethoven came back from the dead he’d be like, “That beat is tight!” I just let the vibes flow in.



Any work in the field?
Yes, I worked for a studio for a while, created their logo and brand.


Design Role:
We had clients come into our studio wanting to get their newest track recorded from us, us being the owner, our beats creator Baseboomer, and my good friend Nate Gibson, the sound engineer. After they recorded a song my part came into play, I’d design their album cover, t-shirt, business card, and anything else they needed. It was pretty fun; I really enjoyed being in there watching and listening to these people just pour their heart out into a song while in the vocal booth. The energy they put out was amazing, makes me think about how awesome it most have been to see Biggie Smalls and all the classic Hip-Hop heroes 'going in' in the booth.


Videography?
I inquired film and editing skills through out my skateboard years, filming my friends and myself skating, uploading the footage, and editing them into skateboard montages. That's what really got me into 'being on the computer' and then finding out how much I enjoy it. I actually thought about going to school for videography. When I joined the studio's team they needed a few more helping hands in the video department so I helped film and edit music videos for clients.

Future?
I want to learn how to create music, I have some good ideas brewing.
